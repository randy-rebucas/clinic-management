# Notifications

The Notifications system keeps staff and patients informed about important events — appointment reminders, lab results, prescription updates, and system alerts.

---

## Accessing Notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation bar. A dropdown shows your unread notifications. Click **View All** to open the full Notifications page.

---

## Notification Types

| Type | Triggered By |
|---|---|
| **Appointment Reminder** | Upcoming appointment for the logged-in user |
| **Appointment Confirmation** | Patient confirmed their appointment |
| **Lab Result Ready** | Lab result status changed to Completed |
| **Prescription Ready** | Prescription has been dispensed |
| **Low Stock Alert** | Inventory item falls to or below reorder level |
| **Expiry Alert** | Inventory item is approaching or past its expiry date |
| **Invoice Payment** | A payment was recorded on an invoice |
| **Queue Update** | Patient status change in the queue |
| **System Alert** | System-level messages from the administrator |

---

## Managing Notifications

### Marking as Read

- Click a notification to open the related record. The notification is automatically marked as read.
- On the Notifications page, click **Mark All as Read** to clear all unread notifications at once.

### Filtering Notifications

On the full Notifications page, use the filter controls to view notifications by:
- Type
- Read / Unread status
- Date range

### Deleting Notifications

Click the **delete** icon on a notification to remove it from your list. Deletion is permanent.

---

## Unread Badge

The bell icon in the navigation bar shows a badge with the count of unread notifications. The count updates automatically.

---

## Push Notifications (Mobile / Browser)

If your clinic has Push Notifications enabled:

1. When you first log in, your browser may ask **"Allow notifications?"**
2. Click **Allow** to receive push notifications even when the app is not the active tab.
3. To disable, click **Disallow** or manage notifications in your browser settings.

Push notifications deliver the same alerts as in-app notifications to your device or browser.

---

## Notification Preferences

Notification delivery channels (email, SMS, push) are configured by your administrator in **Settings → Notifications**. Contact your admin if you need to change how or when notifications are sent.

---

## Patient Notifications

The system can also send notifications directly to patients for:
- Appointment reminders (email or SMS)
- Lab result availability
- Follow-up reminders

These are sent automatically based on the notification settings configured in **Settings → Notifications**.

---

## Tips

- Check notifications at the start of your shift to catch any overnight alerts (lab results, low stock, etc.).
- Critical alerts such as **Out of Stock** and **STAT lab result** notifications should be actioned immediately.
- Administrators can set escalation rules so that unanswered alerts are re-sent or forwarded to another staff member.
